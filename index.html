The feed, A Social Media/Website designed for Designers One of the only projects where coding was involved, would be a project which is called “The Feed”. In this project, a website/ Social Media was designed, for Designers, fashion and entertainment lovers to share, and post about their hobbies and interests. The Great Wave Of Plastic

The great wave of plastic, is a poster design, advocating for climate change and the usage of plastic goods. The reason behind this piece was mostly related to climate change and how a single human can contribute towards climate change, without even knowing. The aesthetic behind the shown piece is inspired by the famous painting by Hokusai “The great wave of Kanagawa”. The piece indicates the great wave, however with a lot of small pieces of plastic. In Fact those pieces of plastic are the weekly plastic usage for an average American resident. This piece was done to indicate the Climate disaster in a more understandable scale for the audience.

Niki Brand

The shown pictures are a few logos and concepts designed for a client for a brand named “NIKI”. As one of the first brand identity projects that I was involved in, was the brand identity for the new company “NIKI”. Since “NIKI Corporation” is a pharmaceutical company, the aesthetics for the logo are simple, and classy, the color palette is a combination of blue and green which are usually dedicated to pharma and health.

Nature Zine
